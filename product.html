Description

The Ligne 2 is the archetypal DuPont lighter — rectangular, palladium-plated, and finished with the house's signature diamond-head guillochage. The lid opens and closes with the cling that made the maison famous. A dual-flame torch ignites reliably in any weather. Fully serviceable at any authorised DuPont atelier.

Care & Service

Every DuPont lighter is designed to be serviced, not replaced. Wipe down with a dry microfibre cloth. Return to any authorised atelier for flint replacement, re-lacquering, or full service. Lifetime after-sales care, regardless of age of piece.

Shipping & Returns

Complimentary insured shipping within the United States. Orders ship next business day from our California distribution centre. Free returns within 30 days on unused pieces in original packaging. Complimentary gift wrap available at checkout.
